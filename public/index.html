For Startups

Did you know that many of today’s schoolchildren will eventually be employed in jobs that have yet to be created? Our environment is changing dramatically but the school system and learning styles have not fully kept up with this evolving world.

We know that changing a whole school system is not easy and takes a very long time, but that process often occurs one student, one family, one school at a time. Therefore, we would like to provide as many possible opportunities for children to learn science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) subjects.

Benesse Silicon Valley is an educational research unit of Benesse Holdings (see: About Us) so we can provide the newest methods and materials for you below our cost (often free of charge). For Educators
